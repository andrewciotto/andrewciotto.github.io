The Problem

Investors will be critical to this next phase of startup innovation and growth. However, becoming an angel and more importantly a successful angel investor is more difficult now than ever before. More text describing the issues with investing in current environment and how angel investors have a hard time finding startups or smth along these lines.

- Lack of compelling deal flow

- High overhead of diligence on deals

- No community, mentors or guidance

How do we solve this problem?

Aquila Capital is effectively building infrastructure to facilitate early-stage investments for angel investors. We provide existing investors [as well aspiring angels] access to high volumes of pre-vetted deal flow + integration into an exclusive investor community.

- Work with accelerators & incubators across North America and funnel their start-ups into our private start-up database.

- Facilitate warm intros between our angels and start-ups they want to invest in.

- Angels in our network also get access to events where world-class speakers [unicron-exit founder, GP at a Fund] will come to educate angels on investment thesis, how to do diligence, etc.
